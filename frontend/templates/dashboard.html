DR Severity Levels
Level 0 - No DR

Level 1 - Mild

Level 2 - Moderate

Level 3 - Severe

Level 4 - PDR

About This System

Team Thiran's Diabetic Retinopathy Detection system uses advanced deep learning to identify signs of DR from fundus images. The system classifies retinas into 5 severity levels to help healthcare professionals prioritize treatment and intervention.
